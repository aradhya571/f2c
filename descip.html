About the product
The bright red coloured and heart shaped Red Delicious litchi are crunchy, juicy and slightly sweet.
Red Delicious Lichi are a natural source of fibre and are fat free. They contain a wide variety of anti-oxidants and polynutrients.


Benefits
Litchi are one of the healthiest fruits. Rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber which keep our digestive and immune system healthy. Protects from Alzheimers, type 2 diabetes and cancer. It is a natural teeth whitener and prevent bad breath. Eating litchi peel lowers the risk of obesity. Lichi mask is an excellent cure for wrinkles.


Storage and Uses
Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Wrap them with newspaper individually to keep them fresh for a long time. If any litchi goes bad, it protects other litchi from getting spoiled. Take a cup of water, dissolve 1/8th teaspoon of salt in it.Soak freshly cut slices and drain them. Now, rinse them in fresh water so that they will not taste salty. Can be eaten as a fresh fruit or used in salads, smoothies, cookies, pies. Roast Litchi peel into a crispy snack.
